God's Math — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Christian App Empire LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the God's Math mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

  • Device & usage data: Basic analytics and crash data to improve stability (if enabled by your device settings).
  • Advertising identifiers: On iOS, we may request App Tracking Transparency permission to show personalized ads through Google AdMob. You may decline and still use the App with non-personalized ads.
  • Purchase data: Apple processes in-app purchases; we receive confirmation that you own the Pro (Remove Ads) product.
  • Local storage: Calculator history, theme preferences, and settings are stored on your device via secure local storage—not on our servers.

How We Use Information

  • Display banner and interstitial advertisements (unless you purchase Pro to remove ads).
  • Deliver optional daily verse notification reminders if you grant permission.
  • Restore Pro purchases and remember your preferences.
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs.

Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information under their own policies:

  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads
  • Apple App Store & In-App Purchase

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • Decline tracking on iOS when prompted (ATT).
  • Disable notifications in iOS Settings.
  • Purchase Pro to remove advertisements.
  • Clear calculation history inside the App.

Data Retention

Data stored on your device remains until you delete the App or clear app data. We do not operate a user account system for God's Math.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information processed by the App. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
